Project Problem Definition

The goal of the project is to evaluate the facial expression of presidential candidates during debates. Using debate videos showing only one candidate at a time we are planning to classify and compare the facial expression and emotion of the speaker and listener. Our target emotions are Neutral, Happy, Sad, Surprise, Fear, Disgust, Anger, Contempt.
